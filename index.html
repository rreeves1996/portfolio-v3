About Me

Growing up, I remember always having a huge interest in tech. My other interest was music & art - I started piano at age 3, and have always been an artist. Both of these interests would eventually naturally coalesce into graphic/UI design. As I got older, I spent my free time learning graphic design and attending camps during the summer for video game programming. And then, with my background that could easily kick-start my career in programming/software development after high school, I did what any other impulsive 18 year old would do: started my career in a completely different field! Since then, I've worked on cars, then switched to brick and stone masonry which I have been in for almost four years. Although I enjoy the hard work and satisfaction of the trades - something I will never be able to replace - I eventually understood that switching to a career in programming would be the best option for me, my body, my brain, and the creative side of my personality.

I quickly dove back into programming. After rekindling my passion for it, I decided to switch careers to something programming-oriented. Today, as I learn more and more about modern tech, software development, and UI/UX design, I find myself incredibly excited to continue my journey in the tech industry. It didn't take long to realize that - just like a trade - I would have the opportunity to learn and hone my programming skills for the rest of my life!

What is my current focus?
