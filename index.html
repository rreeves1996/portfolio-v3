ABOUT

Growing up, I remember always having a huge interest in tech, programming and design. I spent my free time learning graphic design and attending camps during the summer for video game programming. And then, with my background that could easily kick-start my career in programming/software development after high school, I did what any other impulsive 18 year old would do: started my career in a completely different field! Since then, I've worked on cars, then switched to brick and stone masonry. Although I enjoy the hard work and satisfaction of the trades - something I will never be able to replace - I eventually understood that switching to a career in programming would be the best option for me, my body, my brain, and my creativity.

I quickly dove back into programming. After rekindling my passion for it, I decided to switch careers to something programming-oriented. Today, as I quickly approach my graduation from the UW Full-Stack Software Development Bootcamp, I can't help but find myself incredibly excited for the future ahead of me. It didn't take long to realize that - just like a trade - I would have the opportunity to learn and hone my programming skills for the rest of my life!
